Open data applications

Results of inspections of Czech inspection authorities

The app showed results of inspections made by Czech inspection authorities. Contained data linked to the business registry.

Map of Public Contracts

The app showed public contracts, contracting authorities and suppliers on a map and computes basic statistics for each contracting authority and supplier.

Public Budgets

The app showed budgets (financial incomes and outcomes) of Czech municipalities. It provided different statistics and comparisons.
